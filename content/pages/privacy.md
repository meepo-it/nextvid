## Introduction

Welcome to NextVid ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our AI video generation platform at nextvid.ai (the "Service"). By using NextVid, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

- **Account Information**: Name and email address provided when you register.
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the Service, including prompts submitted, models selected, videos generated, and credits consumed.
- **Payment Information**: Billing details processed securely through our payment provider (Stripe). We do not store full card numbers.
- **Device & Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, operating system, and referral source, used for security and analytics.
- **User-Uploaded Content**: Images you upload for image-to-video or reference-to-video generation. These are stored in our cloud storage and used solely to fulfill your generation request.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide, operate, and improve the NextVid platform
- Process video generation requests and manage credit balances
- Send transactional emails (receipts, generation confirmations, support replies)
- Detect fraud, abuse, and security threats
- Comply with legal obligations

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

## AI-Generated Content

Prompts and inputs you submit are sent to third-party AI model providers (such as ApiMart) to fulfill generation requests. These providers operate under their own data processing terms. We recommend avoiding the submission of sensitive personal information in prompts.

Videos generated through NextVid are stored in your account and are accessible only to you unless you choose to share them.

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
- Generated videos are retained for 90 days after creation, after which they may be deleted automatically.
- You may delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting support.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, access controls, and secure cloud infrastructure (Cloudflare) to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

## Third-Party Services

NextVid uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

- **Stripe** — payment processing
- **Resend** — transactional email
- **Cloudflare R2** — file storage
- **ApiMart** — AI model API provider

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Object to or restrict certain processing activities
- Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@nextvid.ai.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.
